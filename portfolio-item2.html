Campaign Trac

January 2018 ||| Skills: Visual Design, Research ||| URL:Campaign Trac

Campaign Trac is to enable Sales Teams and Brand Managers track campaigns – on the fly, as it happens. The product platform offers a mix of data visualization, analytical and export tools to slot insight seamlessly into your workflows.

The developer and marketing team reached out to me for a possible collaboration of designing Campaign Trac’s landing page. This project ended up covering not just the redesign of their website, but the user and admin dashboards too.
