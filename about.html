About Me

Devyani Bhati

Hello! I’m an Interaction Designer from India. I like doing new things thoughtfully. I am very keen on what I do and honesty is always at the core of the work. I sculpt the solid form of design expression that allows the user to interact, learn and feel.

I enjoy working in digital spaces. I work to synthesize and develop designs through research and prototyping. While I enjoy many types of design I have constantly gravitated towards interaction,I also work on wordpress projects, so if you would like to speak to me, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
